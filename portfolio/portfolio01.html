Website for an English Tutor

  • Category : Web Design, UI/UX Design
  • Completion : Dec 2018
  • Client : David Beairsto (English Tutor)
  • Live Link

This project is for my teacher when I was at VGC International College. He asked me to make his own website because he was a tutor as well as a teacher at the school and he wanted to raise his awareness as a freelance tutor.

This website is designed with the image of simplicity, cleanliness, and modernity according to his needs because main target customers have a wide range of age groups such as students who learn English especially IELTS and people who want to improve their public speaking skill.
