Eco-Friendly Clothing Brand

  • Category : Web Design
  • Completion : JUL 2018
  • Client : Re-fa
  • Prototype Link

I had two weeks to learn about Marketing through Social Media when I was a student in the business course at the VGC International College. Deliverables for each group were making a website connecting social media channels that we created each account for this project and giving a presentation introducing our project to examiners.

In my group, we decided to provide Eco-Friendly Clothing, which is made from recyclable fabric as our project. The name of the clothing brand is Re-fa that means a combination of "Recycle and Fabric". Based on the main future that is "Eco-Friendly Clothing, this website is Earth-themed and designed with Earth tone.
